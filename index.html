SAMCIS Organization Membership Drive

Saint Louis University hosts organization drives every year in an effort to foster a supportive campus environment through shared interests, diversity, and a feeling of belonging. This year, SAMCIS students were welcomed by several organizations, including SCHEMA, SICAP, JPIA, ICON, and more.

SAMCIS opens a week-long org membership drive

From August 22 to 26, a multitude of student organization kicked off the academic year's membership recruitment drive with enthusiasm and creativity. They warmly greeted students at Devesse Plaza, offering exciting themes and lively colors. Both school-wide and university-wide organizations, publications, and political parties had their distinctive approaches to attract and welcome new members. They established registration booths showcasing their unique selling points.

Photo Gallery

The membership drive gallery featured a dynamic display of student organizations, offering a glimpse into their unique offerings and inviting new members to join.

SAMCIS Organizations

College involves intense studying, but extracurriculars teach valuable life lessons, connect us with diverse peers, and enrich our growth and community impact.

ICON - Integrated Confederacy

An organization created for the CIS division of SAMCIS to help students with studies and find enjoyment in their chosen computer course. Integrated Confederacy looks to help students through competitions and hackathons and even bring about their creative talent through works such as film and photography.

Visit Facebook Page

JFINEX - Junior Financial Executives

A co-curricular organization of SAMCIS. It is academically focused in promoting, uplifting, and improving the Financial Management education through constant involvement of the members in various activities in line with its core values C.H.R.I.S.T. (Commitment, Humility, Respect, Initiative, Sacrifice and Trust) while embracing the four core values of SLU.

Visit Facebook Page

CMV - Campus Mission Volunteers

An organization established for the Saint Aloysius Gonzaga Parish of SLU, is dedicated in serving our Almighty Father. Compromised by the different ministries under the Formation, Liturgy and Social Action committees, the organization is also geared to spreading His love through different in-school programs by outreach activities for the outside world and thos who are in need especially during calamities, and to encourage the Louisians in serving for the glory of His name.

Visit Facebook Page

JPIA - Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants

Sheltered and guided by the four core values of SLU, JPIA is a co-curricular organization under SAMCIS recognizes its mission of bringing transformative education to its students and cultivating a culture of leadership and concern that extends to the community. In the spirit of "Service Beyond Borders", exists to serve JPIAn Community (internal activities) and the Louisian and outside communities (external activities).

Visit Facebook Page

JPMAP - Junior People Management Association of the Philippines

A co-curricular student organization guided by PMAP having the goal to develop and empower its members to enable them to grow from academic to professional level as future people managers of the society. Leadership starts here. Transformation starts within ourselves. Because in JPMAP, we transform Human Resources into Human Assets.

Visit Facebook Page

KASAMA/SSC - Kataas-taasang Sanggunian ng mga Mag-aaral/Supreme Student Council

A student government organization that is well-founded on prestige and excellence. The assembly plays as the highest student organization in the SAMCID community and serves as a bridge between the students and the administration of the said school of the university.

Visit Facebook Page

LIGHT - Louisian Imbibed with Genuined Spirit for Hospitality Transformation

A co-curricular organization that develops the leadership skills and abilities of its members in preparation for their future careers through various activities and events. These fun-filled activities pushes and inspires Hospitality and Tourism students to exercise their zealous desire to learn and grow to become an effective leader.

Visit Facebook Page

MM - Marketing Mixers

A student organization of future marketing practitioners in SAMCIS and under the affiliation of the PIMA - envisions itself as an outstanding vehicle to develop aspiring marketing professionals by promoting marketing and its importance to the business world through the application of lessons, activities, and updates in line with the university's core values involved marketing students.

Visit Facebook Page

PESO - Progressive Economists' Solidarity

"The few, the proud, and the upper class" - Progressive Economists' Solidarity (PESO) is the mother organization of Business Economics major. Imbued with economic wisdom, PESO, as a catalyst of change, thrives for growth in different aspects, individually and collectively.

Visit Facebook Page

RPG - Rated Production Guild

Rated Production Guild is desirous of developing our talents and knowledge which will entertain as well as educate students if the school and the community, strengthening and the bonds among members and participating in other activities which would benefit the guild and all other compromises it, whether physically, mentally, and/or morally.

Visit Facebook Page

ROTARACT

ROTARACT means Rotary in Action. It is a service club for young people ages 18 to 30 who are dedicated to finding innovative solutions to the world's most pressing challenges while developing leadership skills and making friends from around the world. The club helps to address the physical and social needs of their communities through different projects and activities.

Visit Facebook Page

SCHEMA - Think. Feel. Write

SCHEMA, founded on August 2018, is an independent organization and a recognized publication of the assembly. It is the official student publication of the SAMCIS, which serves as an instrument for journalistic, literacy, and artistic exposure. This also serves as a training ground for students to hone their writing and technical abilities through responsible journalism.

Visit Facebook Page

SICAP - Society of Integrated Commercians for Academic Progress

SICAP is a committed extra-curricular organization of SAMCIS that specifically provides BLT topics done through seminars, aims to help the students to progress academically and the one who pioneers in honoring academic excellence to the qualified SAMCIS students.

Visit Facebook Page

YES - Young Entrepreneurs Society

An organization where Louisian Entrepreneurs would have an avenue to develop and enhance their potentials that is geared towards skills, talents, creativity and innovativeness. It also reinforces and improves the entrepreneurial competencies that would help each individual to become socially World-Class Entrepreneurs.

Visit Facebook Page
